🏭 公司简介 / Company Profile

专业无尘布制造商 | Professional Cleanroom Wiper Manufacturer

深圳源洁无尘布产品有限公司位于中国深圳市龙华区，专业生产无尘布(无尘擦拭布)和工业擦拭布。我们为电子、半导体、汽车和制药行业提供高品质的清洁解决方案。

Shenzhen Yuanjie Cleanroom Products, based in Longhua, Shenzhen, China, is a professional manufacturer of cleanroom wipes and industrial wipes. We supply high-quality cleaning solutions for electronics, semiconductor, automotive, and pharmaceutical industries.
